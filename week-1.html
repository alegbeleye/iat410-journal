Week 1 - Beginning with Unity

January 12, 2026 - 09:30 AM

This week I began working with Unity for the first time in a structured way. I focused on understanding how the editor is organized and how GameObjects and Components work together. I created a simple project, organized folders properly, and experimented with adding Sprite Renderers, Colliders, and Rigidbody2D to objects. One of the most important things I learned was how scripting connects behaviour to objects. I wrote a small movement script using keyboard input and observed how physics affected motion. Seeing a character respond to my code helped me understand how gameplay systems are built from simple interactions. By the end of the week, I felt more confident navigating Unity and understanding how scenes, prefabs, and scripts relate to each other. This foundation will support future prototyping work.
